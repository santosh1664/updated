ICT Web Accessibility

The ICT Web Accessibility platform uses the ASES tool, which checks the compliance of government websites with the Electronic Government Accessibility Model (eMAG 3). The eMAG is a specialized version of the international WCAG document aimed at the Brazilian government, but does not exclude any good accessibility practice from the WCAG. To understand how ASES classifies errors, read the tool's documentation .

Collection of .gov.br pages

TIC Web Accessibility collects websites from a seed (list of initial domains) that are under the “.gov.br” TLD up to at least 5 levels deep from the home page. This makes it possible to collect thousands of public pages on the web.

The ASES tool defines the following compliance bands

The first, which is 95% or more, represents a website/page with few errors. In the range between 85% and 94.99%, the result requires attention. Between 70% and 84.99%, there is a considerable number of errors. Below 70%, the website/page has many errors..

Overview of accessibility on government websites

The TIC Web Accessibility aims to analyze the level of compliance of Brazilian government websites with the Electronic Government Accessibility Model (eMAG) and present an overview of accessibility and the most common errors found on web pages under the .gov.br domain.
